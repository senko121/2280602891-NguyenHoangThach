> This section presents my personal reflections and feedback after completing the internship in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program. The opinions below are based on my own experience and are intended to provide constructive feedback that may contribute to improving future internship programs.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Learning Environment**
The learning environment throughout the internship was highly professional, supportive, and well organized. The FCAJ roadmap was structured logically, allowing participants to progressively build their knowledge from fundamental AWS concepts to advanced cloud services and practical project implementation. The combination of theoretical content, laboratory exercises, and project-based learning created an effective and engaging learning experience.

**2. Support from Mentors and Organizers**
The mentors and program organizers were always enthusiastic and responsive whenever participants encountered technical difficulties. Rather than providing direct solutions, they encouraged independent thinking by offering guidance, helpful resources, and constructive feedback. This approach significantly improved my problem-solving ability and self-learning skills throughout the internship.

**3. Relevance to My Academic Background**
The internship was closely related to my major in Information Technology, particularly in cloud computing, networking, system administration, and software engineering. Many concepts that I had previously studied in university became much clearer after applying them through hands-on AWS laboratories and the final cloud project.

**4. Learning and Professional Development**
The internship provided valuable opportunities to develop both technical and professional competencies. Besides learning AWS services and cloud architecture, I improved my ability to analyze technical requirements, troubleshoot deployment issues, prepare technical documentation, and manage project tasks in a structured manner. These experiences have strengthened both my technical confidence and my readiness for future professional work.

**5. Program Organization and Community**
One of the most impressive aspects of FCAJ was the collaborative learning community. Participants actively shared knowledge, discussed technical challenges, and supported one another throughout the learning process. In addition, the opportunity to participate in technical sharing sessions and activities at the AWS office provided valuable exposure to industry practices and emerging cloud technologies.

**6. Internship Experience and Learning Resources**
Although the internship did not provide financial support or internship allowances, the learning resources, practical laboratory exercises, mentoring sessions, and well-designed learning roadmap were highly valuable. The knowledge, practical experience, and exposure to real cloud technologies were the most meaningful benefits I gained from participating in the program.

---

### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**

The most satisfying part of the internship was completing the entire AWS learning roadmap and successfully applying the acquired knowledge to design, deploy, test, and optimize a practical cloud project. This experience helped me connect theoretical concepts with real-world implementation and gave me greater confidence in working with cloud technologies.

**What do you think the program should improve for future interns?**

The overall internship experience was very well organized. However, the program could become even more valuable by incorporating additional real-world case studies, production-like scenarios, and collaborative team projects. These activities would provide participants with broader exposure to practical cloud engineering challenges.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**

Yes. I would definitely recommend this internship program to students who are interested in Cloud Computing and AWS. The learning roadmap is comprehensive, the laboratory exercises are practical, and the mentors are supportive. It provides an excellent opportunity to gain both theoretical knowledge and valuable hands-on experience within a structured learning environment.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I hope future editions of the program will include more enterprise-level projects, advanced cloud architecture topics, and additional workshops focusing on modern cloud-native technologies and DevOps practices.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**

Yes. I would be very interested in participating in more advanced learning programs or technical communities organized by FCAJ, especially those focusing on advanced AWS services, cloud architecture, and real-world cloud solutions.

**Any other comments (free sharing):**

I sincerely appreciate the effort of the mentors and organizers in designing such a comprehensive learning program. The internship not only expanded my technical knowledge but also strengthened my confidence, self-learning ability, and professional mindset. I believe the experience gained from the First Cloud AI Journey program will serve as a valuable foundation for my future studies and career in Cloud Computing.
