# Summary Report: “FCAJ Community Day”

### Event Objectives

* Share effective learning methods and strategies for maintaining long-term motivation.
* Provide career orientation and professional mindset in the AI era.
* Introduce modern AI-assisted software development workflows.
* Present practical methods for integrating AI into the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC).

### Speakers

* **Huynh Hoang Long** – Shared strategies for maintaining motivation and building sustainable learning habits.
* **Thinh Nguyen** – Introduced Master LLM Prompting techniques and AI workflow optimization.
* **Khang** – Shared experiences about work ethics, integrity and professional development.
* **Thao** – Introduced the BMX methodology for AI-assisted software development.

### Key Highlights

#### Learning psychology and building sustainable habits – Huynh Hoang Long

The first session focused on understanding how the human brain works and how to leverage psychological principles to build sustainable learning habits.

* Learned how dopamine affects daily learning behaviors.
* Understood why social media platforms such as TikTok and online games can easily attract users through instant rewards.
* Applied the concept of **Streak** to maintain daily learning consistency.
* Broke large goals into smaller tasks that can be completed within 10 to 30 minutes.
* Applied the **2-minute rule** to reduce procrastination.
* Designed a suitable study environment and reward system to increase motivation.

#### Master LLM Prompting and AI workflow optimization – Thinh Nguyen

The second session introduced practical techniques for interacting with Large Language Models (LLMs).

* Learned advanced prompting techniques to obtain more accurate AI responses.
* Understood how an AI extension can optimize prompts before sending requests to AI models.
* Explored how AWS services can be integrated to automate prompt optimization.
* Learned how to build reusable AI workflows instead of manually writing prompts repeatedly.
* Understood the importance of controlling context to improve AI response quality.

#### Professional mindset and integrity at work – Khang

The third session focused on professional development and work ethics.

* Understood the importance of integrity in professional environments.
* Learned that AI should not be used to hide knowledge gaps or bypass learning processes.
* Recognized the importance of taking responsibility for one's own work.
* Understood how maintaining a positive mindset can improve long-term performance.
* Learned practical experiences and expectations from employers regarding interns and fresh graduates.

#### BMX methodology for AI development – Thao

The final session introduced a modern approach to AI-assisted software development.

Instead of using a **prompt-first** approach, BMX emphasizes a **document-first** workflow where documentation becomes the central source of truth throughout the project lifecycle.

The workflow includes:

* Brainstorming project ideas.
* Creating a Vision Document (VOD).
* Defining Epics and User Stories.
* Dividing work into smaller tasks.
* Assigning AI agents to implement each task.
* Reviewing and refining outputs continuously.

This approach helps:

* Reduce AI hallucinations.
* Improve context management.
* Increase development consistency.
* Simplify large project management.
* Improve collaboration among team members.

### Key Takeaways

#### Learning Mindset

* Build sustainable habits using the **Streak** strategy.
* Break down large goals into manageable tasks.
* Create a suitable learning environment to maintain consistency.
* Focus on long-term growth instead of short-term motivation.

#### AI Development Mindset

* AI should be treated as a productivity amplifier rather than a replacement for fundamental knowledge.
* Strong foundations are more important than chasing every new trend.
* Prompt optimization can significantly improve AI output quality.
* Documentation is essential for managing AI-assisted projects.

#### Professional Mindset

* Integrity is one of the most important qualities in professional environments.
* Always understand the "Why" before implementing technical solutions.
* Take ownership of personal learning and development.
* Continuously improve problem-solving skills.

#### Modern Software Development

* Adopt document-first workflows instead of prompt-first approaches.
* Divide projects into smaller manageable components.
* Utilize AI agents to automate repetitive tasks.
* Encourage teamwork and collaborative project development.

### Applying to Work

* Apply the **Streak** concept to maintain a consistent daily study schedule.
* Use prompt optimization techniques when working with AI tools.
* Apply the BMX methodology to future projects.
* Create project documentation before implementation.
* Focus on strengthening foundational knowledge instead of following short-term technology trends.
* Collaborate with team members to improve communication and project management skills.
